Chicken

Description:

This delicious chicken recipe is a family favorite. It's easy to make and full of flavor, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a shallow bowl, combine breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken breast into the beaten eggs, then coat with the breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Brown the chicken on both sides.
  5. Transfer the browned chicken to a baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious chicken!
