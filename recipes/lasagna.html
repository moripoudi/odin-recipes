Lsagna

Description:

This classic lasagna recipe is hearty, cheesy, and perfect for feeding a crowd. Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel make it a comforting favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce

Steps:

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef with onion and garlic. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. In a baking dish, layer cooked lasagna noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, Parmesan, and béchamel. Repeat.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
